INTRODUCTION

Hi! I am Atreyi, a 4th year student pursuing Integrated MSc. Physics at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. I have always been interested in astronomy, as my favorite hobby is to look up at the night sky and bore people by identifying constellations and planets. My research interests lie in Multi-Messenger Astronomy and High-Energy Astrophysics. I am currently working with the instrument CZTI Astrosat as a part of STAR Lab at IIT Bombay.

BACKGROUND

I was born in Delhi, but I have spent the majority of my life in Mumbai. I completed my schooling in Navi Mumbai, where I developed my passion for dancing, singing, and poetry writing, achieving recognition and winning prizes in all three disciplines at various stages of my education. Following high school, I relocated to Hyderabad to pursue my Integrated Master's degree.
