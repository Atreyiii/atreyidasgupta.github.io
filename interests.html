MY INTERESTS

AD ASTRA: The Astronomy and Astrophysics club of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad

I am the former President of AD ASTRA and in the club, I have learnt various of things, starting from event management to leadership skills. The club has given me so many things and I will forever be grateful for my time in the club. I led and managed a team of over 50 members in the club. We have a 9.25-inch diffraction-limited Schmidt-Cassegrain telescope which we use for stargazing and astrophotography.

Public Speaking and Outreach

I have a very outspoken nature and I really enjoy public speaking, in the form of outreach of Astronomy. I have given talks in under-privileged schools, interacting with students from grade 9 and 10. I discussed Astronomy as a career option and how to get started with the knowledge of Astronomy. I have also given outreach talk in VIT Chennai on Neutron Star Merger Ejecta. I have been invited by TedX Bits Hyderabad to be a speaker in their Community Meet to share experiences and inspire students with insights on personal and professional growth.
